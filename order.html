Types of Dishes

You have 3 types of food to choose for your meal right from starters to deserts

Starter

A starter is a small quantity of food that is served as the first course of a meal.

Main-course

A main course is the featured or primary dish in a meal of several courses.

Desert

Dessert is a course that concludes a meal,The course consists of sweet foods.
